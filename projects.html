Automated Geometry Theorem Prover

Automatically prove geometry theorems. Accepts a description of a geometric problem/theorem and outputs a proof in english of the geometric proof. Eventually, the plan is to output a machine-verifiable proof (ie., Lean or Coq) and experiment with modern techniques (machine learning, ai, etc).
